About Gadam

Built for the Brave

At Gadam Fire Department, we take pride in our ability to serve our community with unwavering dedication and professionalism. Our team of highly trained firefighters and emergency responders work around the clock to ensure the safety and well-being of our citizens.

Protect Life & Property

Rapid response and rescue operations to safeguard our community from fire, medical emergencies, and natural disasters.

Training & Experience

Continuous training and professional development to ensure our team is always prepared for any emergency situation.

Community Outreach

Educational programs and community events to promote fire safety awareness and emergency preparedness.

Modern Fleet

State-of-the-art equipment and vehicles to ensure effective response to all types of emergencies.
