Volunteer Requirements

Basic requirements to become a volunteer firefighter

Age Requirement

Must be between 18-65 years old and legally eligible to work in the United States.

Physical Fitness

Pass a basic physical fitness test and medical examination to ensure you can handle the demands.

Background Check

Complete a background check and provide references to ensure community safety.

Time Commitment

Commit to a minimum of 4 hours per week for training and emergency response.

Training Completion

Complete our comprehensive training program including firefighting and first aid certification.

Local Residency

Live within a reasonable distance from the fire station for emergency response.

Training Process

What to expect during your volunteer training journey

1
Application

Submit your application and attend an information session to learn more about our program.

2
Screening

Complete background check, physical fitness test, and medical examination.

3
Basic Training

Complete 120 hours of basic firefighter training including classroom and hands-on practice.

4
Certification

Pass final examinations and receive your volunteer firefighter certification.
