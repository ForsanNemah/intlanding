How do I call for a fire emergency?

In case of a fire emergency, immediately dial 911 or our emergency hotline 0761-8523-398. Provide clear information about the location and nature of the emergency.

What if I need a medical emergency?

For medical emergencies, call 911 immediately. Our paramedics are trained to handle all types of medical emergencies and will provide immediate care.

How can I become a volunteer?

Visit our volunteer page to learn about requirements and application process. We provide comprehensive training for all volunteers.

Do you offer fire safety training for businesses?

Yes, we offer comprehensive fire safety training programs for businesses, schools, and community organizations. Contact us to schedule a session.
